About Us

Confucius Institute at Egerton University was established in July 2012 with the joint efforts of Nanjing Agricultural University and Egerton University as the first Confucius Institute with agricultural characteristics in the world. Since its establishment, the institute has carried out a great deal of tasks in Chinese language teaching, cultural exchange between Kenya and China, agricultural vocational and technical training and joint degree programs for postgraduate students. The Institute provides Kenyans with opportunities to learn Chinese language and culture. This blending of Chinese language, culture and arts curriculum with transfer of expertise in modern farming has benefitted research institutions and local communities.
